Zero Impediments to Living

Autonomous Living Technologies (Auli Tech) builds open-source hardware and applications to improve the lives of those with disabilities.

Cato

Head Mounted Mouse

Cato is a hands-free, disability-friendly mouse and keyboard.

It’s a small chip that can attach to the side of a pair of glasses or a hat. Then, using small head motions, a user can control their cursor and input keystrokes.

Lantern

Communication Flipbook

The Lantern is an AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) web and mobile application.

With modular tabs and cards containing text, images, or links, users can either click between cards and tabs with their hands or with any other assistive mouse devices they may use. The cards then speak, and/or navigate to allow the user to communicate their needs.
